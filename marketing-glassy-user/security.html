How we handle PHI

Handling California Workers' Compensation data requires more than just standard encryption. Our infrastructure is specifically architected to recognize and respect Protected Health Information (PHI).

From the moment an IME report or medical bill enters our ecosystem, it is treated with the highest level of sensitivity. Our systems are programmed to minimize the persistence of PHI, ensuring that only necessary data is used for analysis, while the integrity of the legal file remains paramount.
